Muffins

Description

Little cupcakes wrapped in single use paper.

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Cocoa powder
  • Oils
  • Artificial Cherries

Cooking Steps

  1. Add water to maida till its paste-like
  2. Add the artificial cherries
  3. Keep in oven and bake
  4. Wrap in environment harming single use paper
  5. Serve hot when a couple of them are ready
